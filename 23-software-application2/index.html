Tiers are division/segments that roughly represent a range of how well someone or something performs. The skill range is calculated based on other peoples or other objects skill or usefulness level. Tier 1 is the lowest and Tier 7 is (currently) the highest. The Tiers are designated by Roman numerals (I, II, III, IV, V, VI, VII, VIII = 1, 2, 3, 4, 5, 6, 7, 8) etc.
